About Us

Being in a car accident is a terrible experience but, getting treated for one shouldn't be. Which is why many clinics strive to provide the best service possible for their patients. However, with the amount of extra paperwork and billing required, it is not always easy!

That's why MVA Manager aims to take the administrative burden out of Motor Vehicle insurance by tracking appointments, paperwork, and billing without taking a fee-shared approach with a middle-man billing company.

Unlike many of the online clinic management services, MVA Manager solely focuses on tracking patients under Motor Vehicle insurance. Overall, MVA Manager is a web application designed aid clinics manage Motor Vehicle Accident (MVA) patients to ensure the required appointments are schedule and, paperwork is completed in a timely manner.
